Day 6: Hip & Back Reset

Today we return to the Hip & Back Reset. Repeating a familiar reset helps the body recognize the pattern of releasing unnecessary effort through the hips, back, and breath.

Practice builds familiarity, and familiarity makes the reset easier to access when you need it.

Start today's reset

Best experienced with headphones in a quiet space.

Why repeat this reset

The hips and lower back often return to tension when daily stress, posture, or breathing habits begin to build again.

Repeating this reset helps reinforce a more natural pattern of breathing and movement, so physical ease becomes easier to recover.

How to perform the reset

  • Find a comfortable position lying down or seated.
  • Allow your breathing to remain natural.
  • Follow the audio guidance without forcing movement.
  • Notice how the hips and lower back respond as tension reduces.
  • Allow breathing and movement to settle together.

What you might notice afterward

  • Less tightness in the lower back
  • More relaxed hips
  • Smoother breathing through the ribs and torso
  • A greater sense of physical ease
