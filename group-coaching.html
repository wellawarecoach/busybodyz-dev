The BusyBodyz Difference

More than a class. A coached progression.

Each group begins together, trains twice each week, and progresses through a planned 6–8 week experience. The goal is not simply to complete workouts—it is to become more capable in real life.

When Life Interrupts

Missing one session does not mean losing your progress.

Your regular cohort remains your primary schedule. When you cannot attend, you will have a practical path to stay connected to the program.

Option 1: In-studio make-up

Attend one of the available weekly make-up sessions, subject to space and program compatibility.

Option 2: Recorded online session

Complete a recorded version from home when an in-studio make-up is not practical.

The purpose is continuity.

Flexibility supports the structure—it does not replace the value of attending with your group.
