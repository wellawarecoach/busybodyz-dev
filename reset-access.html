The 4 Reset Categories (use the right one for today)

1) Nervous System

Downshift reactivity. Restore baseline calm.

Use when: overstimulated, anxious, “always on,” trouble unwinding.

2) Cognitive

Reduce mental load. Clear attention and decision fatigue.

Use when: brain fog, rumination, overload, can’t focus.

3) Structural

Unload bracing. Improve breathing mechanics and position.

Use when: shallow breathing, neck/trap tension, tight ribcage.

4) Sensory

Expand awareness. Reduce perceptual narrowing from stress.

Use when: hyper-alert, tunnel vision, heightened threat response.

Simple rule

Don’t overthink it. Choose the category that matches your current state. Do one reset. Then go back to your day. Consistency beats intensity.
