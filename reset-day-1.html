Day 1: Attention Narrowing Reset

When your system feels scattered or overloaded, the first step is reducing noise. This reset helps narrow attention, calm the nervous system, and create a clearer starting point for the body.

Start today's reset

Best experienced with headphones in a quiet space.

What this reset helps with

The Attention Narrowing Reset is useful when you feel mentally scattered, overstimulated, or internally busy.

By narrowing attention and reducing unnecessary sensory load, the body often begins to release tension and restore a calmer breathing rhythm.

How to perform the reset

  • Get into a comfortable seated or lying position.
  • Allow your eyes and face to soften.
  • Follow the guidance in the audio without forcing anything.
  • Let your attention narrow naturally.
  • Notice changes in breathing, tension, or mental noise.

What you might notice afterward

  • Quieter mental activity
  • Reduced tension in the neck, jaw, or shoulders
  • Smoother breathing
  • A calmer and more grounded feeling
