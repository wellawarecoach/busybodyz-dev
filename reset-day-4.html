Day 4: Sensory Recovery Reset

As the body settles, the next step is reducing sensory overload. This reset helps the nervous system recover from excessive stimulation and restore a calmer, more organized internal state.

Start today's reset

Best experienced with headphones in a quiet environment.

What this reset helps with

Modern environments constantly stimulate the senses — light, sound, movement, and digital information.

This reset helps reduce that sensory load so the nervous system can recover and settle into a calmer baseline.

How to perform the reset

  • Find a quiet space where you can sit or lie comfortably.
  • Allow your eyes and facial muscles to soften.
  • Follow the guidance in the audio without forcing anything.
  • Allow your senses to gradually settle and narrow.
  • Notice any reduction in tension or internal noise.

What you might notice afterward

  • A calmer sensory environment internally
  • Less mental clutter or overstimulation
  • More relaxed breathing and posture
  • A greater sense of internal quiet
