Day 5: Emergency Reset

Today we revisit the Emergency Reset. Returning to the same reset helps reinforce the body's ability to settle breathing and reduce unnecessary tension when needed.

Consistency is where resets begin to become automatic.

Start today's reset

Best experienced with headphones in a quiet space.

Why repeat this reset

The Emergency Reset is designed to quickly calm the system when tension spikes. Repeating it helps your body become more familiar with shifting out of that state.

Over time, the nervous system begins to recognize the pattern and respond more quickly.

How to perform the reset

  • Find a comfortable seated or lying position.
  • Allow your breathing to remain natural.
  • Follow the audio guidance without trying to control the outcome.
  • Notice areas of tension gradually softening.
  • Allow your attention to remain steady and calm.

What you might notice afterward

  • Smoother breathing
  • Reduced tension in shoulders or neck
  • Greater mental clarity
  • A more settled internal state
